⚡ 2-Minute First Visit
1. Go to fieldiq.biz → enter email → get PIN → log in
2. Tap Log Visit
3. Search and select your hospital
4. Enter your name, territory, visit type
5. Tap 🎙️ — speak about your visit — tap again to stop
6. Tap Process Visit Log
✅ That's it. FieldIQ does the rest automatically.
📍 GPS & Privacy
GPS location is captured only at the moment you submit a visit.
FieldIQ does not continuously track your location.
