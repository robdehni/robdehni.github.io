📖 Quick Start Guide

FieldIQ — Territory Intelligence Platform

← Home
⚡ 2-Minute First Visit
1. Go to fieldiq.biz → enter email → get PIN → log in
2. Tap Log Visit
3. Search and select your hospital
4. Select territory and visit type
5. Tap 🎙️ — speak about your visit — tap again to stop
6. Tap Process Visit Log
✅ That's it. FieldIQ does the rest automatically.
📍 GPS & Privacy
GPS location is captured only at the moment you submit a visit.
FieldIQ does not continuously track your location.
Location data is used only to plot your visit on the territory map.
GPS is captured only when a visit is submitted and is used as proof of presence for field activity reporting.
🤖 What happens after I press Process Visit Log?
FieldIQ sends your voice transcript to AI for analysis.
Within seconds it generates a structured Visit Report including:
→ Visit Summary
→ Key Contacts Met
→ Outcomes & Decisions
→ Next Steps & Action Items
→ Opportunity Signals
→ Priority Level (High / Medium / Low)
Everything is saved automatically to the database. No paperwork required.
1Logging In
Go to fieldiq.biz on your phone or laptop.
Enter your email address and tap Send Login Code.
Check your email for a 6-digit PIN code.
Enter the code. You are now logged in for approximately 24 hours.
⚠️ If a feature stops working, your session may have expired. Go to fieldiq.biz, log in again with a new PIN, then try again.
2Logging a Visit
1. Tap Log Visit from the home screen.
2. Search for the hospital or account name.
3. Select Visit Date, Territory, Visit Type.
4. Confirm your name is displayed correctly. FieldIQ remembers your identity automatically.
5. Tap the 🎙️ microphone and speak naturally about your visit. Tap again to stop.
6. Wait for transcription. Review the text and edit if needed.
7. Tap Process Visit Log. FieldIQ generates your AI report and saves everything automatically.
✅ Speak naturally. You can mention contacts, products discussed, next steps, and sentiment. FieldIQ will extract and structure everything automatically.
3Adding a New Account
If the hospital or account is not in the list, tick ☑ Account not listed.
Enter the Account Name and City.
Complete the visit as normal — record your voice note and process.
The visit is saved with a New Account flag for manager review.
✅ GPS coordinates are captured automatically with your visit.
4Viewing Your Visit History
Tap Visit History from the home screen.
All your visits appear, newest first.
Each card shows: Account, Date, Territory, Visit Type, Priority, and AI Summary.
New accounts are highlighted with a New Account badge.
ℹ️ Visit History is read-only. Contact your manager to correct any errors.
5Priority Levels
FieldIQ automatically assigns a priority to each visit based on your notes.
High — Quotation requested, procurement engaged, strong buying signal.
Medium — Follow-up needed, positive interest, return visit requested.
Low — Informational visit, no current opportunity.
6Your Identity
The first time you use FieldIQ, you'll select your name once from a list.
After that, FieldIQ remembers who you are automatically — you won't be asked again.
Your name will always appear correctly on every visit you log.
