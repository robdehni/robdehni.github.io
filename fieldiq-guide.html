Quick Start Guide
← Home
⚡ 2-Minute First Visit
1. Go to fieldiq.biz → select your name → enter your PIN → log in
2. Tap Log Visit
3. Search and select your account / location
4. Enter visit date, territory, visit type
5. Tap 🎙️ — speak about your visit — tap again to stop
6. Tap Process Visit Log
✅ That's it. FieldIQ does the rest automatically.
📍 GPS & Privacy
GPS location is captured only at the moment you submit a visit.
FieldIQ does not continuously track your location.
Location data is used only to plot your visit on the territory map.
GPS is captured only when a visit is submitted and is used as proof of presence for field activity reporting.
🤖 What happens after I press Process Visit Log?
FieldIQ sends your voice transcript to AI for analysis.
Within seconds it generates a structured Visit Report including:
→ Visit Summary
→ Key Contacts Met
→ Outcomes & Decisions
→ Next Steps & Action Items
→ Opportunity Signals
→ Priority Level (High / Medium / Low)
Everything is saved automatically to the database. No paperwork required.
1Logging In
Go to fieldiq.biz on your phone or laptop.
Select your name from the Who are you? dropdown on the home screen.
Enter your PIN when prompted.
You are now logged in. Tap Switch User on the home screen any time to log in as someone else.
⚠️ If you're logged in as the wrong person, tap Switch User on the home screen, then select the correct name and enter your PIN.
2Logging a Visit
1. Tap Log Visit from the home screen.
2. Search for the account name.
3. Select Visit Date, Territory, Visit Type.
4. Tap the 🎙️ microphone and speak naturally about your visit. Tap again to stop.
5. Wait for transcription. Review the text and edit if needed.
6. Tap Process Visit Log. FieldIQ generates your AI report and saves everything automatically.
✅ Your name is already locked in from login — Log Visit no longer asks who you are. Speak naturally about contacts, products discussed, next steps, and sentiment. FieldIQ will extract and structure everything automatically.
3How to Get Better Reports
Speak naturally — there is no script to follow.
A 30–90 second recording is usually enough.
The more specific your information, the better your report.
1. Who did you meet? Mention names whenever possible, plus job titles or roles.
2. What was discussed? Briefly explain the purpose of the visit.
3. What feedback was received? Customer reactions, concerns, objections, or interest.
4. What decisions were made? Any agreements, requests, commitments, or approvals.
5. What opportunities were identified? Potential business, future discussions, trials, evaluations, orders, projects, etc.
6. What are the next steps? Follow-up actions, responsibilities, and agreed dates.
❌ Weak example: "Visited customer. Good meeting. Will follow up next week."
✅ Strong example: "Visited ABC Account. Met Sarah, Operations Manager. Discussed the new product range. Customer requested pricing and agreed to review next Tuesday. Potential opportunity for approximately 50 units per month."
The intelligent system can only capture information that is mentioned during the recording. The more specific your information, the more accurate your reports, action items, opportunity signals, onboarding intelligence, and future handover reports will be.
4Adding a New Account
If the account is not in the list, tick ☑ Account not listed.
Enter the Account Name and City.
Complete the visit as normal — record your voice note and process.
The visit is saved with a New Account flag for manager review.
✅ GPS coordinates are captured automatically so the account appears on the Visit Map.
5Viewing Your Visit History
Tap Visit History from the home screen.
All your visits appear, newest first.
Each card shows: Account, Date, Territory, Visit Type, Priority, and AI Summary.
New accounts are highlighted with a New Account badge.
ℹ️ Visit History is read-only. Contact your manager to correct any errors.
6Priority Levels
FieldIQ automatically assigns a priority to each visit based on your notes.
High — Quotation requested, procurement engaged, strong buying signal.
Medium — Follow-up needed, positive interest, return visit requested.
Low — Informational visit, no current opportunity.
✅ Managers can change priorities from the Dashboard.
7Identity Lock Rule
Your name is selected once at login and stays locked across the whole app — Log Visit, Visit History, and every page reads it automatically.
This ensures your visits are grouped correctly in the Dashboard and Reports.
⚠️ If you're logged in as the wrong rep, use Switch User on the home screen to fix it before logging a visit.
8For Managers
Open the Dashboard to see KPIs, rep performance, territory coverage, and opportunity signals.
Managers are automatically redirected to the Manager Portal immediately after successful login. Dashboard, Open Actions, Visit Map, AI Insights, Log Visit, Visit History, and Quick Start Guide are accessed from the Manager Portal.
Reps cannot access manager-only pages by typing the URL directly; they are blocked or redirected back to their own rep screen.
New accounts pending review appear with a New Account badge in Visit History.
