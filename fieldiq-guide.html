Getting Started with FieldIQ

Eight simple steps from login to a logged visit.

1

Select your name and enter your PIN

Go to fieldiq.biz, choose your name from the list, and enter your assigned PIN to log in.

2

Tap Log Visit

From the home screen, tap the big blue Log Visit button.

3

Select Hospital or Account

Search and select the hospital you visited, or tick "Account not listed" to add a new one.

4

Record your visit using the microphone

Tap the mic and speak naturally about your visit. Tap again to stop recording.

5

Review and edit transcript if needed

Your speech is transcribed automatically. Tap the text box to correct anything before processing.

6

Tap Process Visit Log

FieldIQ sends your transcript for analysis and generates a structured report.

7

AI creates summary and saves visit

Your visit is saved automatically, complete with summary, priority, and next steps. No paperwork required.

8

View history and activity from Visit History

Tap Visit History from the home screen to see all your logged visits, newest first.

Roles & Security
👤

Sales Rep

Can log visits, view their own Visit History, submit new accounts, and generate AI visit reports. Cannot switch users or access manager-only pages.

⭐

Manager

Can do everything a Sales Rep can, plus access the Dashboard, review new accounts, and switch between users for administration and support.

📍

GPS location is captured only at the moment you submit a visit, as proof of presence. FieldIQ does not continuously track your location.

⚠️

Do not share your PIN with other users. Each PIN identifies you personally on every visit you log.
